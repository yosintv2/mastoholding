Our Foundation

From Vision to Reality

Masto Holdings was born from a bold vision: to build a diversified portfolio of world-class businesses rooted in Nepal, reaching global markets. Founded in 2026, we set out to create a conglomerate that doesn't just pursue profit, but delivers genuine value to every stakeholder.

Our founders saw an opportunity to harness Nepal's growing economy, strategic location, and talented workforce to build enterprises capable of competing on the global stage. From day one, sustainability and ethical business practices have been woven into our DNA.

Today, Masto Holdings encompasses 8 portfolio companies across diverse sectors — from craft beverages to digital technology, from sports entertainment to real estate — each operating with excellence and a commitment to creating positive impact.
