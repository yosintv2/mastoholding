Everything you need to know about Masto Holdings

Masto Holdings is a diversified investment and holding company established in 2026, focused on creating sustainable businesses and delivering long-term value across technology, media, beverages, transportation, sports, and digital platforms.

Masto Holdings is headquartered in Nepal with operations spanning across South Asia and international markets. We have regional offices in major business hubs supporting our portfolio companies.

We invest across diverse sectors including Drinks & Breweries, Technology & Software, Digital Platforms, Transportation, Sports & Entertainment, Real Estate, and Investments. We also have a dedicated Innovation Lab for future ventures.

We believe in building businesses that create long-term value, not just short-term profits. Our approach focuses on operational excellence, innovation, sustainability, and creating positive impact for stakeholders.

You can reach us through our Contact page with inquiry forms for media, partnerships, careers, or general inquiries. We also provide office locations and communication channels.
