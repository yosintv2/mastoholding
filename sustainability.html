Our Commitment

Sustainability at the Core of Everything We Do

At Masto Holdings, sustainability isn't a corporate checkbox — it's the foundation of our investment philosophy. Every business decision is evaluated through an ESG lens, ensuring that our growth creates positive outcomes for all stakeholders.

We believe that the most successful businesses of the future will be those that balance profitability with responsibility. Our portfolio companies are required to meet rigorous environmental and social standards from day one.

From reducing carbon footprints to championing diverse workforces, we are actively working to make Masto Holdings a model for responsible business in South Asia and beyond.

Framework

Our ESG Pillars

E — Environmental

Planet

We are committed to minimizing environmental impact. This includes reducing carbon emissions, managing waste responsibly, conserving natural resources, and investing in clean technologies.

  • Carbon footprint reduction programs
  • Renewable energy transition plans
  • Zero-waste manufacturing goals
  • Sustainable supply chain standards

S — Social

People

Our social commitment centers on creating equitable opportunities for all. We invest in employee well-being, community development, fair labor practices, and diversity across every level.

  • Employee wellness & development
  • Community investment initiatives
  • Gender & diversity equity targets
  • Fair compensation frameworks

G — Governance

Principles

Strong governance ensures we act with integrity and accountability. Our framework includes transparent reporting, ethical conduct standards, anti-corruption policies, and independent oversight.

  • Independent board oversight
  • Annual ESG reporting
  • Anti-corruption & ethics policies
  • Transparent stakeholder communications

In Action

Key Initiatives

01

Green Operations Program

A company-wide initiative to audit and reduce energy consumption, transition to renewable sources, and eliminate single-use materials across all Masto portfolio operations by 2027.

02

Nepal Community Investment Fund

A dedicated fund committing 2% of annual profits to education, healthcare, and economic development programs in the communities where our businesses operate.

03

Women in Leadership Program

A targeted mentorship, training, and sponsorship initiative designed to accelerate women into senior leadership and board positions across all Masto companies.

04

Responsible Supply Chain

A rigorous supplier assessment program ensuring that every partner in our supply chain meets minimum environmental and labor standards before onboarding.
